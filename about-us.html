How It All Began

A timeline of our fragrant evolution

2010

The Spark

Founder Ayesha Khan, a perfumer's daughter, began blending scents in her Lahore kitchen, capturing the essence of Pakistan's vibrant bazaars and Himalayan valleys in small batches.

2013

First Breakthrough

Our signature scent "Fleur" won the Artisan Perfumery Award, putting ZephyrLuxe on the global fragrance map with its unique blend of Pakistani rose and Himalayan cedar.

2016

The Atelier

Opened our first boutique perfumery in Lahore, where customers could create custom scents using our library of 150+ rare essences from around the world.

2019

Global Recognition

Featured in Vogue International as one of "10 Fragrance Houses Changing the Scent Landscape" for our sustainable sourcing practices and innovative compositions.

2022

New Horizons

Launched our first international flagship in Paris, bringing Eastern perfumery traditions to Western audiences with our critically acclaimed "Silk Road Collection".

Today

The Journey Continues

With a team of 12 master perfumers and partnerships with sustainable farms across 3 continents, we continue to craft scents that tell stories and evoke emotions.

Our Guiding Principles

What makes ZephyrLuxe different

Sustainable Sourcing

We partner directly with small farms using regenerative agriculture, paying premium prices for exceptional quality botanicals while supporting ecosystems.

Artisan Methods

Each fragrance undergoes 27 separate quality checks and ages for 6-8 weeks to achieve perfect harmony, just like fine wine.

Emotional Alchemy

We don't just create perfumes - we craft emotional landscapes. Each scent is designed to evoke specific memories and feelings.
