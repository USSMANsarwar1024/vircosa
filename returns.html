Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Zephyr Luxe purchase. Please review our policy details:

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase
  • Products must be unopened and in original condition
  • A return shipping label will be provided upon approval
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving your return
  • Sale items are eligible for exchange or store credit only
  • Perfumes that have been used or opened cannot be returned for health and safety reasons
